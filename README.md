# 📄 AI Ethics Assignment — Designing Responsible and Fair AI Systems


This assignment demonstrates our understanding of key AI ethics principles and how to apply them in practice. We analyzed real-world case studies, matched ethical frameworks, and carried out a fairness audit on the COMPAS dataset using AI Fairness 360. We also reflected on how to design fair, transparent, and human-centric AI systems.

What was achieved:

Identified and explained core concepts like algorithmic bias, transparency, and explainability.

Analyzed two case studies: a biased hiring tool and facial recognition in policing — proposing solutions to mitigate harm.

Audited the COMPAS dataset for racial bias, visualized disparities, and suggested remediation steps.

Reflected on applying ethical principles in future AI projects.

Developed a clear policy guideline for ethical AI use in healthcare, covering patient consent, bias mitigation, and transparency.

Through this assignment, we strengthened our ability to build AI systems that are fair, responsible, and trustworthy — vital skills for ethical AI practice.
